"Nistipata"

Description

The source of nutrition for those in a tough financial place, like broke students and addicts.

Consists of macaroni, minced meat and ketchup, every item the cheapest of its kind.

Ingredients

  • Minced meat | 400g
  • Macaroni | 400g
  • Ketchup | whatever is left in the bottle

Steps

  1. Boil the macaroni until it is cooked to your liking. 8 minutes is a good starting number.
  2. Fry the minced meat until it is brown and ready.
  3. Mix the two ingredients in the pot you used to boil the macaronis.
  4. Add ketchup and enjoy!
