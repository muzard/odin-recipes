Breakfast

Description

My easy go-to breakfast.

You will hopefully find all the ingredients in your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • Coffee grounds
  • Water
  • Milk or cream
  • Butter
  • Bread
  • Ham
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Put your coffee grounds and water into your coffee maker and switch it on
  2. Get your milk or cream on the table, as well as the topping for your sandwich
  3. Butter your bread and place the toppings
  4. Pour your coffee into your mug and add milk or cream to your liking
  5. Sip on your coffee until you're late from work
